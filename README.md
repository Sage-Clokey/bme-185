# BME 185 — Biotechnology Technical Writing & Science Communication

Technical writing, research, and science communication deliverables from BME 185 at UC Santa Cruz. The course trains scientists to communicate complex biotechnology topics clearly to technical and non-technical audiences.

## Final Technical Report

**"Who Owns Your DNA? The Case for Personal Genetic Ownership"**

A full technical report arguing for individual ownership of personal genetic data. Covers:
- Current legal landscape: GDPR, GINA, state-level genetic privacy protections
- Risks of third-party ownership: insurance discrimination, law enforcement database use, corporate data exploitation
- Ethical frameworks: autonomy, informed consent, beneficence
- Policy recommendations for federal genetic privacy legislation
- Case studies: 23andMe data breach, ancestry database use by police, biobank ethics
